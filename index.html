whereAmI

Did you know that you are being watched and tracked almost every time you click a button or type a key on the internet? We are an open source project to help you identify and take back control over where and who has your data. You should be able to browse, game, and enjoy the internet without giving away your privacy and protections. Click the button below to start regaining control over your digital life.

Your activity on the internet is monitored almost from the first key stroke. The more add-ons and permissions that you allow, the more your information is being collected.
Companies collect this data through the submissions to their website (ex. Facebook/Instagram), cookies, key logs, and local data storage. The permissions you allowed gave them ownership and permission of your data.
Your data is sold off to other companies at a massive profit, because hey... you might even have paid for that app or website subscription. And the cycle starts over again, and again, and again.
